BROOKLYN

Brooke Abdullah (known by her stage name Brooklyn) is an American singer and songwriter who aims to touch the world with her charismatic personality and undeniably unique vocals. Born and raised in Hyattsville, Maryland, she would have to fend for herself at an early age after her dad left her mom at the tender age of 3 due to her moms mental illness. Her mom would then bounce around from jail to mental institutions which would force Brooklyn to find a way to survive. As Brooklyn grew older she performed in various talent shows and pageants. She also was a lead vocalist in a local band (Vertical) and in her schools choir from elementary throughout high school. All of this prepared Brooklyn to have the confidence to begin professionally recording in the studio and post videos via social media to gain recognition and support from her peers both locally and worldwide. She has drawn comparisons to artist such as Beyoncé, Mariah Carey and Keri Hilson. Using social media as an important career outlet, Brooklyn has captured the attention of music legends such as Missy Elliott. Brooklyn most recently released her new break out single “Take It There” and plans on releasing her untitled debut EP along with new songs and visuals during the early portion of 2017.
